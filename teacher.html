Mrs. Leonard

Ms. Leonard is a dedicated high school technology teacher with a passion for inspiring students in the fields of graphic design, computer science, and web design. With a strong background in technology and education, she strives to create an engaging and supportive learning environment where students can develop critical problem-solving skills and explore their creativity. In addition to teaching, Ms. Leonard leads the W.I.S.E. (Women in Science and Engineering) club and the Cinematography Club, providing students with opportunities to expand their interests beyond the classroom. She is committed to preparing students for future careers in STEM and helping them develop the skills needed to succeed in an increasingly digital world.
